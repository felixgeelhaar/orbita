
## Identity & Authentication

User authentication system with OAuth2, encrypted token storage, and user profile management. Foundation for all user-facing functionality.

---

## Billing & Entitlements

Stripe integration for subscriptions, module (Orbit) entitlement management, and payment processing. Controls access to premium features.

---

## Productivity Core (CORE Framework)

Task management with Capture, Organize, Review, Execute workflow. Includes task creation, prioritization, duration estimates, and completion tracking.

---

## Scheduling Engine

Central scheduling service that consumes candidates from tasks, habits, meetings, and projects. Applies hard constraints and soft penalties, aligns with Ideal Week templates.

---

## Smart Habits

Habit tracking with adaptive scheduling. Learns optimal times for habit execution, generates sessions, and adjusts based on completion patterns.

---

## Smart 1:1 Scheduler

Intelligent meeting coordination for recurring 1:1s. Manages frequency, finds optimal slots, and coordinates across calendars.

---

## AI Inbox Processing

AI-powered inbox for capturing and classifying items. Automatically categorizes, prioritizes, and routes items to appropriate contexts.

---

## Automations Engine

User-defined automation rules with triggers, conditions, and actions. Enables workflow automation without coding.

---

## Time Insights & Analytics

Analytics dashboard with time tracking, productivity metrics, goal progress, and trend analysis. Provides actionable insights.

---

## Calendar Integrations

Multi-provider calendar sync (Google, Outlook, Apple). Bi-directional sync with conflict detection and resolution.

---

## Wellness Sync

Health and wellness tracking integration. Syncs with fitness devices and apps to incorporate wellness data into scheduling decisions.

---

## CLI Interface

Primary command-line interface using Cobra. All productivity operations available through intuitive CLI commands.
